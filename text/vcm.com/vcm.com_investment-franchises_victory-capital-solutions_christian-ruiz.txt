Christian Ruiz 









Christian Ruiz 
Analyst






Christian Ruiz is an Analyst for the VictoryShares and Solutions team. In his role, he structures data insights to help support management in decision making across investment research and data analysis, while providing assistance to the team’s portfolio managers.
Mr. Ruiz joined Victory Capital in 2022. Prior to becoming a member of the VictoryShares and Solutions team, he worked as a licensed lending officer for two and a half years as well as a consultant for one and a half years. As a Data Analyst and Process Improvement Consultant he was responsible for streamlining operations and the development of algorithmic solutions to solve complex accounting and financial problems for clients.

Mr. Ruiz holds a B.B.A in Management Science with a Minor in Statistics and a certificate in Supply Chain and Operations Management from the University of Texas in San Antonio.
