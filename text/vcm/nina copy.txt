Nina Gupta
Chief Legal Officer and Head of Human Resource Administration

Prior to joining RS, Ms. Gupta worked at BlackRock Institutional Trust Company N.A., where she was deputy general counsel of BlackRock Institutional Trust Company, responsible for providing legal advice in connection with corporate matters, counterparty trading, transactional documentation, and regulatory issues in connection with a variety of different fund structures and accounts.

Previously, Ms. Gupta was an associate at the law firm of Shearman & Sterling LLP in New York and Menlo Park, where her practice focused on general corporate, structured finance and banking matters. Ms. Gupta earned a law degree from the University of London in 1996 and a Masters in Law from the University of Cambridge in 1997.
