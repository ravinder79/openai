Kevin Bales, CFA
Associate Portfolio Manager






Kevin Bales, CFA®, CRPC® is an Associate Portfolio Manager for the VictoryShares and Solutions team. In this role he is focused on the firm’s multi-asset, multi-manager, active quantitative and exchange-traded fund business. The VictoryShares and Solutions team is responsible for providing custom advice and solutions to clients across a variety of channels and portfolio types.
Mr. Bales joined Victory in 2018 and has 16 years of investment experience. Prior to becoming a member of the VictoryShares and Solutions team, he was a Director of Manager Research for Hartland & Co., an institutional consulting firm.
Mr. Bales earned his Bachelor of Business Administration degree in finance from Cleveland State University and earned his Master of Business Administration degree in finance from John Carroll University. He also holds the Chartered Financial Analyst® and Chartered Retirement Planning Counselor® designations.
